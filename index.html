Frequently Asked Questions

How do hemorrhoids occur?

Hemorrhoids occur when the veins around the anus or lower rectum become swollen and inflamed.

How often can I use the wipes?

TenderTouch Wipes can be used as often as needed, particularly after bowel movements, to ensure cleanliness and comfort. They are safe for regular, daily use.
